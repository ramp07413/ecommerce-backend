Enterprise-level e-commerce platform banane ke liye, aap kuch aur features aur unke corresponding routes add kar sakte hain. Aapke current routes ko dekhte hue, yahan kuch suggestions hain:

**Customer Features:**

*   **Gift Card Routes:** Gift cards khareedne aur redeem karne ke liye.
*   **Subscription Routes:** Products ke subscription-based orders ke liye.
*   **Advanced Search Routes:** Filters, sorting, aur suggestions ke saath ek powerful search ke liye.
*   **Social Login Routes:** Google, Facebook, etc. se login/signup ke liye.
*   **Saved Address Routes:** Users ko multiple shipping addresses save karne ke liye.
*   **Back-in-Stock Notification Routes:** Out-of-stock products ke liye notification subscribe karne ke liye.

**Marketing & Promotions:**

*   **Advanced Promotions Routes:** "Buy One Get One" jaise complex promotional rules ke liye.
*   **Affiliate Marketing Routes:** Affiliate partners ko track aur manage karne ke liye.
*   **Customer Segmentation Routes:** Targeted marketing ke liye customer groups banane ke liye.

**Content & Support:**

*   **CMS Routes:** Blog posts, static pages (About Us, Contact Us), aur banners manage karne ke liye.
*   **Support Ticket Routes:** Customer support queries ko manage karne ke liye ek ticketing system.

**Admin & Operations:**

*   **Advanced Logistics/Shipping Routes:** Third-party shipping providers ke saath integration aur shipment tracking ke liye.
*   **Reporting/Analytics Routes:** Sales, customer behavior, aur inventory ke upar detailed reports ke liye.
*   **Audit Log Routes:** Admin aur users ke important actions ko track karne ke liye.
*   **Role Management Routes:** Employees ke liye specific permissions ke saath different roles (e.g., "Warehouse Manager", "Support Agent") banane ke liye.
*   **Multi-Vendor Routes (Marketplace):** Agar aap ek marketplace bana rahe hain jahan multiple sellers apne products bech sakte hain, to unke liye alag se routes.

In routes ko add karke aap apne project ko aur scalable aur feature-rich bana sakte hain.
